Introducing Cellular

The Cellular base theme for Drupal 7 simplifies front-end development of Drupal websites by using the theme layer to control functionality that doesn't require interaction with the database. It handles things like providing lightweight social media links, updating jQuery & jQueryUI, a custom front-end framework, and lots of other fun stuff useful to themers that Drupal makes a pain in the a$$ by default...
